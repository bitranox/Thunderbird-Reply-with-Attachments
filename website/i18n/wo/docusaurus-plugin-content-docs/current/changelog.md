## Coppite yi

Ngir jaar-jaar bi mat ak dëgër-dëgër, seetal ci repo bi
[CHANGELOG.md ci GitHub](https://github.com/bitranox/Thunderbird-Reply-with-Attachments/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md).

- 2.3.2: "Boole nataali ci biir" leegi dafay dellaq nataali yi ci biir téxta bu tontu bi muy URI u done bu base64 (ñakk ndigal: ON); yokkug kalite bu kóodu bi ak yambaley test yi gën a yaatu.
- 2.3.1: Daŋkay taxawal pièces-joynt yi ginnaaw bi Thunderbird tëdd xëtub evenmaan bi ci ginnaaw; yokkoo na hooks yu debug yu mottali ngir wuutu jafe-jafe yi.
- 2.3.0: Siggil na dindi-duppal yi ci pièces-joynt yi, yambaley test yi gën a yaatu, te dindi na ndigali yu jeex nañu ngir soppiku yoon yu AMO yi.
- 2.1.0: Ndimbalug internasyonalisaasioŋ bu mat ngir 100 làkk yi gëna jëfandikoo
- 2.0.0: Bindaat ci sumb bu mat bu am jafe-jafe yépp (EN/DE)
- 1.0.1: Jàll na ci messages.listAttachments()
- 1.0.0: Sottu bi jëkk
