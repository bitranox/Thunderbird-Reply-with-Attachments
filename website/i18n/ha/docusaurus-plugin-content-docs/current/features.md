## Fasali {#features}

- Yana haɗa fayiloli daga asalin imel ɗin ta atomatik lokacin amsawa.
- Halayya mai iya daidaitawa: haɗe‑haɗe na iya
  - a ƙara su ta atomatik, ko
  - a ƙara su ne kawai bayan tabbatarwa (ƙaramin taga mai sauƙin amfani). A cikin Options
    za ka iya kunna tabbatarwar kuma ka zaɓi amsar tsoho (Eh/A'a).
- Jerin toshewa (blacklist) na sunayen fayil (glob patterns) yana hana wasu takamaiman fayiloli a
  haɗuwa ta atomatik. Misalai: `*intern*`, `*secret*`, `*passwor*`.
  Daidaitawa ba ta kula da manya/ƙananan haruffa ba kuma tana duba sunan fayil kaɗai; ba da tsari ɗaya
  a kowane layi a cikin Options.
- Gargadin blacklist (na zaɓi, an kunna ta tsoho): lokacin da jerin toshewarka ya ware fayiloli,
  ƙaramin taga na modal yana jera fayil ɗin da tsarin da ya dace. Mai dacewa da yanayin duhu (Dark‑mode)
  kuma ana iya sarrafa shi da madannai (Enter/Esc don rufewa).
- Yana aiki tare da Reply da Reply all. Wannan ƙarin (add‑on) baya canza Forward.
- Yana ƙara haɗe‑haɗen asali ko da ka riga ka haɗa wani; yana guje wa maimaituwa ta sunan fayil.
- Kariya ta maimaituwa a kowane shafi (per‑tab) tana hana ƙara abu sau biyu a cikin shafin rubuta saƙo ɗaya.
- Ta tsoho tana tsallake takardun shaida na S/MIME don guje wa haɗe‑haɗe marasa buƙata.
- Yana haɗa hotunan cikin rubutu (tsoho: ON). Hotunan da aka dasa ana mayar da su kai tsaye a cikin
  jikin amsa a matsayin base64 data URIs, suna kiyaye tsarin cikin‑layi na asali. Kashe wannan a
  cikin Options don tsallake hotunan cikin rubutu gaba ɗaya.

## Yadda Ake Aiki {#how-it-works}

- Lokacin amsawa, ƙarin (add‑on) yana jera haɗe‑haɗen asali.
- Yana tacewa ya cire sa hannun S/MIME daga haɗe‑haɗen fayil; hotunan cikin rubutu ana mayar da su a jiki (sai dai idan an kashe).
- Na zaɓi yana neman tabbatarwa (mai dacewa da amfani da madannai).
- Yana ƙara fayilolin da suka cancanta a rubutunka, yana guje wa maimaitawa ta sunan fayil.
- Duba “Dalilin da ya sa ana iya kasa ƙara haɗe‑haɗe” a cikin Usage don yanayin gefe.

Lura kan sirri: Dukkan sarrafawa yana faruwa a gida a cikin Thunderbird. Ƙarin (add‑on) baya yin buƙatun hanyar sadarwa a bango.
