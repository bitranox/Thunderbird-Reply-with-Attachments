## Daminɛn dɔgɔya

:::important Thunderbird versiyon kɔfɛ
Add‑on nin bɛ dɛmɛ Thunderbird **128 ESR walima min kura**. Versiyon min kɔrɔw tɛna dɛmɛ.
:::

:::note Tɛlɛmɛtri tɛ; rɛzo bɔyɔ tɛ
Add‑on nin tɛ jɔsi ɲɛrɛyɔrɔ/telemetry, o tɛna ɲini rɛzo bɔyɔ kɔnɔ. Rɛzo sɔrɔli bɛ kɛ kelen de waati i ka klikili linkw furaw kan (Docs, GitHub, Donate).
:::

---

### Instali

1. Instali add‑on nin ka taa Thunderbird Add‑ons la.
2. Suguya: Ka konfirmasiyɔn daminɛ (Options → “Ask before adding attachments”).
3. Suguya: Ka blacklist ɲɛsininkɛ bɛ daminɛ (default): “Warn if attachments are excluded by blacklist”.
4. Suguya: Tɔgɔli blacklist fɔyɔrɔw (lin kelen kelen), misali:

```
*intern*
*secret*
*passwor*  # matches both “password” and “Passwort” families
```

Hakɛ: “# …” sisan kɔfɛ ye yiriwɔrɔ ye dokumɛnti nin kɔnɔ; i tɛ na yiriwɔrɔw fara fɔyɔrɔw kɔnɔ min i bɛ bɔ Options kɔnɔ. Ka fɔyɔrɔ kelen don lin kelen kelen la nikan.

Sisan ka jawa kɔrɔbɔ min bɛ ni piyɛs‑jɔniw (attachments) — originaaliw bɛ na sɔrɔ otomatikimɛn walla konfirmasiyɔn dɔgɔya kɔfɛ b’a fɛ. N’i ye faili dɔw dɔ b’a la jɔsi i ka blacklist la, i bɛ mɔn ɲɛsininkɛ dɔgɔya dɔ min bɛ a tɔgɔw kan.

---

### K’a lajɛ {#verify}

- Ka jawa kɔrɔbɔ dɔ ni piyɛs‑jɔni 1–2, o kɔfɛ ka konfirmɛ ko originaaliw na tɛgɛ i ka compose window kɔnɔ.
- Ka baarakɛcogo sɛbɛn, ka [Konfigurasiɔn](configuration) dɔ (konfirmasiyɔn toggoli, jaabi default, blacklist fɔyɔrɔw).

---

### K’a lajɛ blacklist ɲɛsininkɛ {#verify-blacklist-warning}

- Ka jawa kɔrɔbɔ min bɛ ni faili dɔ kama “secret.txt”.
- N’i daminɛ “Warn if attachments are excluded by blacklist”, dialog dɔ dɔgɔ bɛ fara failiw minnu b’a la jɔsi ni fɔyɔrɔ min bɛ a ɲɛ ye.

N’i tɛ mɔn ɲɛsininkɛ, i b’a lajɛ ko fɔyɔrɔ ye faili tɔgɔ la ka dafa (faili tɔgɔ dɔ nikan, majuskul/minuskul tɛ sɛbɛ). Ka taa Konfigurasiɔn → Blacklist.

---

### Keyboard hakɛ {#keyboard-note}

- Konfirmasiyɔn dialog bɛ na Y/J don Awɔ la ni N/Esc don Ayi la. Kɔnɔka dɔw minnu tɛ Latin ye, ɲɛta kɛw bɛ se ka variye; Enter bɛ konfirmɛ butɔn min bɛ sɔrɔ (focus) la.
