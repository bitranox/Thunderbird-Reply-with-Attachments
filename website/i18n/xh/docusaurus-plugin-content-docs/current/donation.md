## Ukubonakala koNikelo (ukumiswa kweentsuku ezingama‑90)

Isongezelelo siquka uphawu olulula olufihla izikhumbuzi zonikelo okwethutyana emva kokuba unikele.

- Indawo yokuyifumana
  - Iinketho → Icandelo leNkxaso: uya kubona iqhosha elithi “Ndinikile” kunye nendawo encinane yengcebiso.
  - Incoko yokuqinisekisa ukuthumela ikwabônisa iqhosha lokunikela; liyafihla ngokuzenzekela xa ukumiswa kusebenzayo.

- Indlela esebenza ngayo
  - Ukucofa “Ndinikile” kufihla amaqhosha onikelo kunye nezikhumbuzi ezinxulumene noko iintsuku ezingama‑90.
  - Isalathiso semeko sibonisa “Ifihlwe de kube ngu YYYY‑MM‑DD” (ngomhla wendawo yakho). Kukwakho iqhosha elithi “Bonisa uNikelo kwakhona” ukubuyisela ukubonakala kwangoko.
  - Emva kweentsuku ezingama‑90, iqhosha loNikelo liba libonakala ngokuzenzekela kwakhona.

- Ubumfihlo & ukugcinwa
  - Isongezelelo sigcina i-timestamp enye kugcino lwasekhaya lweThunderbird ukukhumbula ixesha lokumiswa. Isitshixo: `donateHideUntil` (i-millisecond ze-epoch).
  - Olu seto lwendawo yiprofaile yakho yeThunderbird (aluhambelani nelifu). Akukho zicelo zenethiwekhi ezenziwa yile mpawu.

- Ukusombulula iingxaki
  - Ukuba uNikelo lusabonakala kanye emva kokucofa “Ndinikile”, linda kancinci okanye vula kwakhona iphepha leNketho; i-UI ihlaziya kwakamsinya nje ukuba useto lugcinwe.
  - Ukuze usete kwakhona ngesandla, cofa “Bonisa uNikelo kwakhona”. Ungalinda ude umhla okhankanyiweyo kwengcebiso udlule.

Le mpawu yeyokwenza kube lula kuphela; ayisoze ivimbe ukusebenza kwesongezelelo kwaye ayiqokeleli nayiphi na idatha yabucala.
