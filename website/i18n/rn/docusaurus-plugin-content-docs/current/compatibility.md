## Ubuhuza {#compatibility}

:::important Verisiyo ya Thunderbird ntoya isabwa
Aka gashirwako gashigikira Thunderbird **128 ESR canke hejuru yayo**. Verisiyo zishaje ntizishigikirwa.
:::

- Thunderbird 128 ESR canke izisumba iyo birashigikirwa (128.x+).
- Verisiyo za Thunderbird zishaje ntizishigikirwa.
- Vyageragejwe kuri Windows, macOS, na Linux.

:::warning Manifest V2 ntishigikirwa
Manifest V2 ntishigikirwa. Aka gashirwako kagenewe **Manifest V3 (MV3)** kuri Thunderbird 128 ESR+.
:::

---

## Gukemura ingorane {#troubleshooting}

- Nimba aka gashirwako kadakora nk'uko witeze, emezako ukoresha verisiyo ya Thunderbird ijanye (128 ESR canke hejuru yayo).
- Raba ikonsole y'amakosa ya Thunderbird (**Tools > Developer Tools > Error Console**) kuraba ingorane zijanye n'aka gashirwako.
- Nimba amahinduwo yabitswe asa n'uko atakoreshwa neza, subira utangure Thunderbird maze wigerageze kandi. (Thunderbird ishobora kubika uko ibintu vyifashe hagati y'amasesiyo; gusubira gutangura bituma amahinduwo mashasha yinjirwa neza.)

## Amakimbirane azwi {#known-conflicts}

- Nta na kimwe kizwi muri iki gihe. Nimba ubona inyomeko zisubirako kenshi canke kunanirwa hamwe n'utundi tugashirwako, turamenyeshe uduhe n'intambwe zo kubisubiramwo.

---

## Beta/Nightly {#beta-nightly}

- Ubwubatsi (builds) bwa Thunderbird Beta na Daily (Nightly) busanzwe bushigikirwa, ariko impinduka zo hejuru zoshobora rimwe na rimwe kubangamira ingene aka gashirwako gikora. Nuhura n'ibibazo, turamenyeshe kandi ushireko verisiyo nyayo ya Thunderbird (urugero: “Daily 131.0a1 2025‑09‑10”).
- Wahuye n'isubira inyuma (Beta/Nightly regression)? Bimenyeshe uduhe n'intambwe zo kubisubiramwo — raba [Ubufasha](support) — kandi ushiremwo umurongo wa build wa Thunderbird nyawo.
