## Liste ya mbongwana

Mpo na lisolo mobimba mpe na maloba ya bososoli nyonso, talá ya répository
[CHANGELOG.md na GitHub](https://github.com/bitranox/Thunderbird-Reply-with-Attachments/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md).

- 2.3.2: "Include inline pictures" sik’oyo etyaka bilili ngámbo na nzoto ya eyano mpenza lokola base64 data URIs (default: ON); bobongoli ya kalite ya code mpe bokolisi ya test coverage.
- 2.3.1: Ebateli ba pièces jointes ata soki Thunderbird ekómi idle na background event page; ebakisi ba debug hooks ciblés mpo na kosilisa mikakatano.
- 2.3.0: Bobongisami malamu ya kolongola ba doublons ya pièces jointes, bokolisi test coverage, mpe bolongoli ba permissions oyo esilaki ntina mpo na kotosa mibeko ya AMO.
- 2.1.0: Lisungi ya internationalisation mobimba mpo na minɔkɔ 100 ya likoló
- 2.0.0: kokoma lisusu na version na makoki nyonso (EN/DE)
- 1.0.1: ebongwani na kosalela messages.listAttachments()
- 1.0.0: libimisi ya liboso
