## Kokokani {#compatibility}

:::important Vɛrsion ya nse (minimum) ya Thunderbird
Add‑on oyo esungi Thunderbird **128 ESR to likoló**. Ba vɛrsion ya kala esungami te.
:::

- Thunderbird 128 ESR to ya sika koleka esungami (128.x+).
- Ba vɛrsion ya kala ya Thunderbird esungami te.
- Emekami na Windows, macOS, mpe Linux.

:::warning Manifest V2 esungami te
Manifest V2 esungami te. Add‑on etindami na **Manifest V3 (MV3)** na Thunderbird 128 ESR+.
:::

---

## Kosilisa mikakatano {#troubleshooting}

- Soki add‑on esali te ndenge okanisaki, yeba malamu ozali kosalela vɛrsion ya Thunderbird oyo ekokani (128 ESR to likoló).
- Talá console ya libunga ya Thunderbird (**Tools > Developer Tools > Error Console**) mpo na makambo etali add‑on.
- Soki ba paramètres ebombami emonisami lokola eza ko sala malamu te, bandisa lisusu Thunderbird mpe meka lisusu. (Thunderbird akoki kobomba état na ba session; kobandisa lisusu esalisaka ete ba paramètres ya sika echargemá malamu.)

## Ba‑konflik toyebi {#known-conflicts}

- Moko te toyebi na ntango oyo. Soki omoni ba attachments ezongelaka mbala‑mbale to mabunga elamwisaka na ba add‑on mosusu, s.v.p. lapola yango elongo na “steps to reproduce”.

---

## Beta/Nightly {#beta-nightly}

- Ba build ya Thunderbird Beta mpe Daily (Nightly) esungamaka mingi, kasi mbongwana uta na likoló ekoki ntango mosusu kobebisa ndenge add‑on esalaka. Soki okutani na mikakatano, lapola yango mpe bakisa vɛrsion ya Thunderbird ya solo (ndakisa, “Daily 131.0a1 2025‑09‑10”).
- Omoni regression na Beta/Nightly? S.v.p. lapola yango elongo na “steps to reproduce” — talá [Lisungi](support) — mpe bakisa chaîne exacte ya build ya Thunderbird.
