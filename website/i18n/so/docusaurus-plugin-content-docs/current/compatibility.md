## Iswaafaqid {#compatibility}

:::important Nooca ugu yar ee Thunderbird
Kordhintu waxay taageertaa Thunderbird **128 ESR ama ka cusub**. Noocyadii hore lama taageero.
:::

- Thunderbird 128 ESR ama ka cusub waa la taageeraa (128.x+).
- Noocyadii hore ee Thunderbird lama taageero.
- Waxaa lagu tijaabiyey Windows, macOS, iyo Linux.

:::warning Manifest V2 lama taageero
Manifest V2 lama taageero. Kordhintu waxay beegsanaysaa **Manifest V3 (MV3)** ee Thunderbird 128 ESR+.
:::

---

## Ka xallinta dhibaatooyinka {#troubleshooting}

- Haddii kordhintu aanay u shaqayn sida la filayo, hubi inaad isticmaalayso nooc la jaan qaadi kara oo Thunderbird ah (128 ESR ama ka dambeeya).
- Ka hubi konsolka khaladka ee Thunderbird (**Tools > Developer Tools > Error Console**) wixii arrimo la xiriira kordhinta.
- Haddii dejimaha la kaydiyey ay u muuqdaan in aanay si sax ah u dhaqan gelin, dib u bilow Thunderbird oo mar kale isku day. (Thunderbird waxa laga yaabaa inuu kaydiyo xaaladda kulamada; dib u bilaabistu waxay xaqiijisaa in dejimaha cusub la raro.)

## Khilaafaadka la yaqaan {#known-conflicts}

- Waqtigan xaadirka ah wax la yaqaan ma jiraan. Haddii aad aragto lifaaqyo si isdaba-joog ah u laba-laabma ama fashilaadyo lala kulmo marka la isticmaalayo kordhino kale, fadlan soo sheeg adigoo ku daraya tillaabooyinka lagu soo celin karo.

---

## Beta/Nightly {#beta-nightly}

- Noocyada dhismaha ee Thunderbird Beta iyo Daily (Nightly) guud ahaan waa la taageeraa, balse isbeddello ka yimaadda ilaha kore mararka qaarkood waxay jebin karaan hab-dhaqanka kordhinta. Haddii aad dhibaatooyin la kulanto, fadlan soo sheeg oo ku dar nooca saxda ah ee Thunderbird (tusaale, “Daily 131.0a1 2025‑09‑10”).
- Ma ku dhacday hoos-u-noqosho (regression) ku jirta Beta/Nightly? Fadlan ku soo sheeg adigoo ku daraya tillaabooyinka lagu soo celin karo — eeg [Taageero](support) — oo ku dar xadhigga dhismaha saxda ah ee Thunderbird.
